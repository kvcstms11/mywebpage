The data submitted through the contact form is used solely to respond to your inquiry. The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR — legitimate interest in responding to contact requests.

Form submissions are processed via FormSubmit.co, which forwards the data to an email inbox. No data is stored on the website itself.

6. Data Retention

Contact form data is retained only as long as necessary to respond to the inquiry, then deleted.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. You may also lodge a complaint with the Austrian Data Protection Authority (Datenschutzbehörde, dsb.gv.at).

8. Contact

For any data protection inquiries, use the contact form on the main page.
